Applying is as easy as...

1

Submit the initial application materials:

  • Application form
  • Application fee: $50 (via check or Bill Pay)
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Signed Student Recommendation Release form
  • Student Recommendation form (K only)
  • Teacher Recommendation Request (Incoming 5-8)
  • Copy of past 2 years Standardized Test Scores & Report Cards (Incoming 1-8)

For 2023-24 Kindergarten, children should turn 5 years old by December 1, 2023.

2

Zion Lion Day

For incoming grades 1-8:

Zion Lion Days are scheduled for students applying to 1st through 8th grades. Applicants will spend a full school day getting to know the students in their grade and the Zion school environment by shadowing a classroom buddy. Applicants will also complete a Math and Reading assessment during their Zion Lion Day. Assessments aid in determining your child's academic placement at Zion.

For Kindergarteners:

Kindergarten applicants will complete a Kindergarten readiness assessment as part of the Kindergarten application process. This will be scheduled after all Kindergarten application materials are received. We take pride in making the assessment fun for the students.

3

Receive your admissions decision

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

K ONLY: Our priority Kindergarten application deadline for 2022-23 is December 31, 2022. We will notify you of our Kindergarten admissions decision in mid-February 2023. Kindergarten applications received after December 31, 2022 will be reviewed on a rolling basis. If accepted, you can reserve your child's seat in the class by submitting a deposit that will be applied toward your child's tuition.
