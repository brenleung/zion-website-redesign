Schedule a Tour!

We offer intimate personal school tours, rather than large group tours, Monday through Friday. We have found that personal tours allow for your individual questions, specific to your child and family, to be answered. Virtual tours will take place at 9:00 am. You will learn about Zion's school community and gain a greater understanding of Zion's goal to educate the whole child.

Note: Please allot approximately 1 hour for your tour.

To schedule a tour, use one of the three follow methods:

  • Email admissions@zionsf.org
  • Call 415-221-7500 ext. 205
  • Use the form below.
