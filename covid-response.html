Zion's COVID-19 Protcols

As of the 2022-23 school year, Zion Lutheran is open for instruction!

The health and safety of our school community are at the forefront of all planning and activities. To ensure that our community remains safe, all school families have agreed to uphold the COVID Community Covenant during this school year. Please feel free to read the guidelines from the California Department of Public Health and/or our school community covenant.

For the latest school-adopted guidelines, refer to Principal Lee's ongoing newsletter.
