High School & Beyond

Starting from middle school, our teachers are committed to a high level of student achievement, performance, and behavior, allowing Zion graduates to be fully prepared in entering academically rigorous college-prepatory high school programs.

To help with the process, we offer a High School Information Night for our eighth graders and their respective families. This to help make the application process go as smoothly as possible, as this process requires effort from both the students and parents. We also strive to give choices to our graduating students. The majority of our students apply to 4-5 schools and are accepted into more than one.
