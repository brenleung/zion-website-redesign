Zion Lutheran School

Live. Lead. Love. Learn.

Established in 1947, our school provides a faith-based, rich, and rigorous education for children from kindergarten through 8th grade in the heart of the Richmond neighborhood of San Francisco.

SCROLL FOR MORE

A community united.

Our teachers and students work together as a community of learners, enlivened by a love of learning and a dedication to personal growth and academic rigor. We dedicate ourselves to helping each child discover and develop their gifts and talents as students, artists and athletes in a caring and compassionate environment that balances freedom with responsibility and service.

Like-minded goals.

The teachers and staff at Zion are dedicated to providing a quality Christian education, to help students excel at math, reading, and writing skills, and to provide them with a solid foundation for high school. However, our goals for our students go beyond this. These goals include:

Live for Christ

Lead a life of godliness, wellness, and integrity

Love and serve one another

Learn for a lifetime
